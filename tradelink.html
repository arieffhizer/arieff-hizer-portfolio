Introduction

Tradelink is a SaaS-based transport management system (TMS) web application designed for clients in the US and Mexico. It offers functionality similar to AllWaysTrack and AppSheet, but with a more intuitive interface and enhanced features. The platform enables both delivery clients and carrier companies to track tasks in real time, streamline logistics, and manage cross-border operations with ease. It also allows company admins to update worker information, record expenses, and facilitate reimbursement processes efficiently.

With its powerful tools and user-friendly design, Tradelink empowers businesses to simplify operations, improve transparency, and achieve greater efficiency in transport management.

Job Scope

    My task was to create a landing page based on their branding and to market and advertise their projects that have been done for their target markets. This landing page are also designed with a single call to action, which is to directly contact their representative for enquiry and request for quotation.

Goals

To introduce the company and service provided, highlighting their completed projects, and to increase future client engagement.

Brief & Research

    After I sit down with the client and was given the brief, I did my research on the brand and collected examples to best highlight the services and completed projects.

Design Stage

    I produced two sets of design for the client to choose from. Based on their feedback I did some iterations on the design.

Final Handoffs

I sent my Figma file to the developer and currently under development for the clients to use as one of their marketing materials.
