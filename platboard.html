Introduction

Introducing PlatBoard, your all-in-one solution for managing multiple crypto wallets effortlessly. With the ever-expanding landscape of cryptocurrencies, keeping track of various assets across different platforms can be daunting. That's where PlatBoard comes in. Designed with simplicity and convenience in mind, PlatBoard consolidates all your crypto wallets into a single, intuitive interface. Say goodbye to juggling multiple apps and hello to streamlined crypto management with PlatBoard.

Issues

  • Complexity in management
  • Difficulty in assessing overall portfolio performance
  • Inefficiency in tracking transactions

Goals

Platboard provide users with a single interface to manage multiple crypto wallets easily. It also streamline the process of tracking transactions, balances, and portfolio performance.

Information Architecture

Information architecture (IA) showing the visual representation of the Platboard's infrastructure, features, flow and hierarchy.

Design System

Defining all the basic elements in advance helped to design fast and consistent throughout the project. Style changes later on were easy to orchestrate.

Key Takeaways

Platboard has emerged as a leading platform for efficiently managing multiple crypto wallets, offering users a seamless and secure solution to navigate the complexities of the digital asset landscape.

By consolidating various wallets into one intuitive interface, Platboard streamlines management processes, enhances security measures, and optimizes user experience.
